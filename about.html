About

The Redemption Power House Ministry is dedicated to reinstating and restoring individuals' destinies to their initial state. We concentrate on imparting correct doctrines, guiding individuals towards obedience to Christ and leading them on the path of redemption. Additionally, through the varied offices in the Spirit, we embrace the fullness of the grace bestowed upon us by our Lord Jesus Christ. This ministry is shepherded by Reverend Prosper Lanya and Pst.Grace Liberty.
